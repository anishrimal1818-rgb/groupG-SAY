If You're Struggling Right Now

If you or someone you know is in crisis, please reach out to these resources immediately.

Samaritans

Free, confidential emotional support 24/7

Call 116 123

NHS Mental Health Crisis

England's crisis helpline for urgent support

Call 0800 011 6117

CALM

Campaign Against Living Miserably - male mental health

Call 0800 58 58 58

Crisis Text Line

Text-based support for anyone in crisis

Text HOME to 85258
